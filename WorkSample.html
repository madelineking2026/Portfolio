I got this data from the Supreme Court Database created by Pennsylvania State University and Washington University. After downloading the data sheet from the database, I first cleaned the data by creating a column for the year each case was decided. Then, I created pivot tables to compare the total number of cases per year to the number of First Amendment cases per year and find the percent of unanimous votes on First Amendment cases per year.
